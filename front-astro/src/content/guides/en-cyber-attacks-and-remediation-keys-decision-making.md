<p>ANSSI publishes a set of guides on remediation, laying out the principles for the management and implementation of remediation within an organisation affected by a cybersecurity incident.</p><p>The purpose of this strategic-level document is to define the main concepts necessary to understand the role of decision makers in the remediation process.</p>
