<p>Digital technologies have become daily partners at work and at home. They provide incredible opportunities as well as sophisticated and destructive threats. Cyberattacks can jeopardize the survival of the organization or seriously compromise its image and its reliability. This encourages organisations to rethink themselves and to adopt a continuous improvement approach.</p><p>It is now impossible to ignore these issues and we do our best to guide each actor through this process, whatever their size, activity, maturity or resources.</p><h3>Controlling digital risk: an appropriate approach</h3><p>This guide, proposed by ANSSI and <a href="https://www.amrae.fr/">AMRAE</a> (a French association of risk and insurance specialists), provides managers and risk managers with a progressive approach to build, Step by Step, a digital risk management policy within their organization.</p><p>The proposed approach makes it possible to:</p><ul><li>TAKING A READING OF THE DIGITAL RISK</li><li>UNDERSTANDING THE DIGITAL RISK AND GETTING ORGANISED</li><li>BUILDING YOUR SECURITY BASELINE</li><li>MANAGING ONE'S DIGITAL RISK AND ENHANCING ONE'S CYBERSECURITY</li></ul>
